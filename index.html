Hacker Heaven

Hacker Heaven is a competition I create for high school students at Cincinnati's annual TechOlympics. To win, students need a basic knowledge of client-side web development, SQL injection, and password hashing. In previous years I sent out packets over a network at the competition and had students intercept and "decrypt" them using Wireshark. Note! The above link has sound (turn your speakers down.
